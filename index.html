How It Works

Betstack Pro is a sports-betting community: free daily tips, paid bet tips, and membership perks. We charge for picks and access — we never hold your stake or guarantee a result.

Getting started
📝
1 · Create an account
Register with your details, verify you're 18+, set a withdrawal PIN, and pick a membership plan. Browsing tips is free.
🎯
2 · Follow free predictions
Three daily predictions are posted in the Arena before kickoff, with results marked openly — wins and losses both.
🎟️
3 · Unlock bet tips
Unlock a tip and get every selected game — teams, markets and odds. Rebuild the slip at any bookmaker in your country and stake your own money. You keep 100% of what you win — we're paid only for the tip.
💸
4 · Top up & withdraw
Credit is used to unlock tips. Unspent credit is yours and withdrawable anytime; withdrawals are protected by your 4-digit PIN.
How bet tips work

Each tip is a list of selected games — teams, markets and odds — that you can rebuild at any bookmaker in any country. We sell the selections as information. You place and fund every bet yourself on your own account — we never take custody of your stake or your winnings.

Membership tiers

A tier is a membership that unlocks more features (more daily tips, VIP “sure” specials, faster support, a badge). It is not an investment, makes no return promises, and never takes a cut of your winnings.

Fair play & transparency
✅
Results stay public
Every prediction and settled slip keeps its win/loss on the record. A strong history is never a promise of future results.
🔓
Your money stays yours
We don't pool, lock, or bet your funds for you. You pay for picks and access; betting happens on your own bookmaker account.
Responsible gambling

Betting carries real risk and you can lose money. Outcomes are never guaranteed. Set limits, never chase losses, and only stake what you can afford to lose. Must be 18+. If gambling stops being fun, take a break or use the self-exclusion tools in Settings → Responsible Gambling.
