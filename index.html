How It Works

Betstack Pro is a sports-betting community: free daily tips, paid booking codes, and membership perks. We charge for picks and access — we never hold your stake or guarantee a result.

Getting started
📝
1 · Create an account
Register with your details, verify you're 18+, set a withdrawal PIN, and pick a membership plan. Browsing tips is free.
🎯
2 · Follow free predictions
Three daily predictions are posted in the Arena before kickoff, with results marked openly — wins and losses both.
🎟️
3 · Buy booking codes
Unlock a code for a fixed price, open it at the listed bookmaker, and stake your own money. You keep 100% of what you win — we're paid only for the code.
💸
4 · Top up & withdraw
Credit is used to unlock codes. Unspent credit is yours and withdrawable anytime; withdrawals are protected by your 4-digit PIN.
How booking codes work

A booking code is a short reference you enter at a bookmaker (Bet9ja, SportyBet, 1xBet, Betway…) to instantly load a pre-built bet slip. We sell the code as information. After unlocking it, you place and fund the bet yourself on your own account — we never take custody of your stake or your winnings.

Membership tiers

A tier is a membership that unlocks more features (more tips, faster support, a better win split, a badge). It is not an investment, makes no return promises, and never restricts withdrawing your own funds.

Fair play & transparency
✅
Results stay public
Every prediction and settled slip keeps its win/loss on the record. A strong history is never a promise of future results.
🔓
Your money stays yours
We don't pool, lock, or bet your funds for you. You pay for picks and access; betting happens on your own bookmaker account.
Responsible gambling

Betting carries real risk and you can lose money. Outcomes are never guaranteed. Set limits, never chase losses, and only stake what you can afford to lose. Must be 18+. If gambling stops being fun, take a break or use the self-exclusion tools in Settings → Responsible Gambling.
